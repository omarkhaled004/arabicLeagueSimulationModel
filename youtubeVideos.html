Let's start our journey together!

Here are the steps to follow:

  1. The attached links are playlists that fully explain our curriculum from scratch.
  2. Go through them in order as attached(if you have a background you may jump through them but we prefer to follow the steps).
  3. In the first step you will learn some islamic concepts in Arabic.
  4. In the second and third steps you will go deeper in Arabic words such as fruits, vegetables, family members and so on.
  5. In the last step you will watch Arabic short stories, this enhances your level by learning Arabic sentences.
  6. Then you can go further through the other steps to enhance your level more.
  7. Lastly, after each step you may go to worksheets page to practice on what you have learned and check your level, and remember that Practice Makes Perfect
  8. By the end of these video you will have a strong level in speaking, listening, writing and reading Arabic.
